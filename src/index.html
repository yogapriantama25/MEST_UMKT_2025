About

The 9th Mechanical Engineering, Science and Technology (MEST2025) hosted by the Institute of Strategic Science and Technology (ItSAT), Universitas Muhammadiyah Kalimantan Timur (UMKT) extends an invitation to prospective authors from universities and diverse organizations to contribute papers within the realm of computational science, mechanical engineering, service and product design, manufacturing, materials sciences, data sciences and digital transformation, architecture technology and related interdisciplinary technology domains.

The main purpose of this International Conference is to bring industry professionals, experts, academicians, and scholars to discuss their expertise for better collaboration in their field. Authors representing universities and various entities are encouraged to submit their research papers. The conference serves as a platform for scholarly exchange, offering swift presentations of articles encompassing experimental, numerical, and theoretical investigations that illuminate pivotal subjects in the fields of technology. Contributions span from intriguing and impactful research with immediate industrial applications to advanced educational resources, technology dissemination, and best practices.

MEST2025 is the ultimate conference that joins the broad field of engineering science that is a broad discipline that encompasses many different scientific principles and associated mathematics that underlie engineering. The conference integrates technology and sciences with the arts, humanities, social sciences, and the professions to tackle the most demanding challenges and advance the well-being of global society.

Review Policy

Peer Review Process

The submitted papers will be evaluated (initial review) by the track director to check the suitability of the papers with the conference's focus and scopes. Afterward, all papers that have passed the initial review by the track director, will be single-blindly reviewed by two reviewers before the presentations. The authors are given a chance to submit the revision no later than 7 days after the conference date. Once they pass the presentation, then the articles will be sent to the committee for content review before collected to be submitted to the publisher.

Initial Review

The track director evaluates each manuscript in the submission track to determine if its topic and content is suitable for consideration for the conference before being reviewed. Manuscripts that do not meet minimum criteria are returned to the authors, by changing the status to rejected. This is in the best interest of the authors who could then decide to either correct the problems or to resubmit the manuscript to a more appropriate venue, avoiding delays from a lengthy review process that would nonetheless lead to another rejection.

Peer Review

Manuscripts that pass the initial review by the Track Directors, will be sent to referees based on their expertise. Each manuscript is reviewed by at least two referees. The referees are asked to evaluate the manuscript based on its originality, soundness of methodology, impact to design research, and relevance to design practices. To facilitate timely publication, referees are asked to complete their reviews and provide individual critiques within two weeks. After collecting the referees' reports, the Track Director makes a recommendation on the acceptability of the manuscript.

Acceptance Decision

Based on the referees' comments, the Track Director makes a final decision on the acceptability of the manuscript and communicates to the authors the decision, along with referees' reports. The status reports to reviewers should identify the reviewers of each paper, the final decision can be "Accept Submission", "Revisions Required", or "Decline Submission." The revised version should be submitted considering the review comments.

Plagiarism

The authors should make sure that the manuscript pass a criterion of less than 25 percents similarity to other publications. The publisher, however, has the right to reject the submission according to the policy of the publisher.
